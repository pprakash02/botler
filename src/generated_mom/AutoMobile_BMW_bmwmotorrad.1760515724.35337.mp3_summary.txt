Main Topic/Objective  
The customer is planning to purchase a 2021 BMW in Kolkata and wants information about routine service requirements, costs, and a service quotation before the vehicle is officially transferred to his name.

Key Points Discussed  
- The customer asked about typical routine service items (coolant change, oil and air filter replacement, engine oil) and whether they need to be performed annually.  
- The service advisor confirmed that standard service includes checks/replacements of the air filter, diesel/fuel filter, and AC filter, and that the exact schedule depends on the vehicle’s service history.  
- The customer provided the provisional vehicle registration number (WB 12 BX 2684) and indicated that the ownership transfer will be completed next week.  
- The service advisor requested a contact method (email or WhatsApp) to send an approximate service quotation.  
- The customer supplied a WhatsApp phone number (partial digits given as 8942 … 93 6138) and agreed to receive the quotation there.  
- There was some confusion about a “bike number” that the service advisor could not locate; both parties acknowledged the mistake and agreed to disregard it.

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- Customer will confirm and send his full WhatsApp/email contact details to the service advisor.  
- Service advisor will prepare an initial service quotation based on the provided registration number and send it to the customer via WhatsApp/email.  
- Once the vehicle registration is officially transferred, the customer will schedule a routine service appointment with the Kolkata OSL BMW Service Center.
