Main Topic/Objective  
The customer (Madam) reported a problem with her washing machine – specifically a broken glass component – and arranged for a service technician to inspect the unit and discuss repair costs.

Key Points Discussed  
The customer initially contacted the service center after a complaint about a washing machine left at a previous location.  
The representative confirmed that a service request had been logged and asked for the customer’s phone number, which the customer provided (9820‑07‑2‑...‑6767).  
The representative explained that a technician would visit the customer’s location within two to three days, later confirming the visit would occur the next day after the Sunday morning prayer.  
A service charge of 1,000 (currency units) will be applied for the technician’s diagnosis of the glass part.  
The actual cost of the replacement glass will be determined after the technician inspects the machine and checks the Whirlpool brand specifications.  
If the part needs to be ordered, the customer will be informed of the additional cost; the service charge remains separate from the part cost.  
The representative clarified that the customer can either provide the part herself for installation (paying only the service charge) or have the service center supply and install the part (incurring both the service charge and the part price).  
The customer expressed concern about being charged multiple times and sought confirmation that charges would be transparent.  
Both parties agreed that the technician’s visit and cost details would be communicated the following day.

Decisions Made or Action Items  
The technician is scheduled to visit the customer’s residence the next day (after Sunday morning prayer) to assess the broken glass component.  
The service center will charge a 1,000 service fee for the diagnostic visit.  
After inspection, the service center will provide the customer with a quotation for the replacement glass and any additional parts required.  
The customer will be informed of the final part cost and instructed on payment procedures before any parts are ordered or installed.  
Both parties will reconfirm the schedule and cost details in a follow‑up call the next day.
