Main Topic/Objective
The conversation is about arranging outdoor advertising on auto‑rickshaws in Delhi (specifically the Paharganj area) for a short‑term promotional campaign tied to a five‑ to six‑day religious journey.

Key Points Discussed

- Pricing is negotiated; the sales representative states the cost is one hundred rupees per auto, rejecting the customer’s suggestion of eighty or fifty rupees.  
- The advertising will be installed in Paharganj but can appear on autos that travel throughout Delhi.

- Communication will continue via WhatsApp, and the sales rep gives a company helpline number for future contact.  
- The customer inquires about who will create the design; the sales rep says they can confirm whether design services are available and will get back shortly.  
- Both parties agree that the ads will be placed on autos that pick up passengers in the designated area.
