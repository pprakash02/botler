Main Topic/Objective  
Discussion between Alok Singh (sales representative) and Baldev (potential buyer) regarding the purchase of industrial/ commercial plots in the M3M project (sectors M9‑M11) near Gurgaon, including details of the development, pricing, payment terms, and investment merits.

Key Points Discussed  
- Location and connectivity: the site is an eight‑minute drive from Sector 88B, near Pataudi Expressway, IMT Manesar, and NH 8.  
- Project size: a 150‑acre ecosystem comprising 38 acres of residential/commercial, 18 acres of green zone, and 50 acres of high‑industrial plots; total of 250 units, with only 250 industrial plots available.  
- Plot sizes: ranging from 182 sqm to 1,800 sqm (approximately 202 sq yd to 2,100 sq yd).  
- Pricing: company’s quoted rate is ₹1.25 lakh per sqm, with an inaugural discount of ₹15,000 bringing it to ₹1.10 lakh per sqm (≈₹92,000 per sq yd). The “starting price” was reiterated as ₹1.25 lakh per sqm.  
- Payment plan: 50 % payable at booking, the remaining 50 % due at possession (estimated 1.5‑2 years after booking).  
- Expected hand‑over: possession anticipated within 18–24 months.  
- Rental yields: current market rents in IMT Manesar cited at ₹40‑45 per sq ft (low) and up to ₹75 per sq ft (high), translating to a monthly rental of roughly ₹4.5‑5 lakh for a 400 sq ft plot.  
- Investment comparison: Alok contrasted the project with Omex 19B, DLF Phase 1, and Reliance‑Jhajjar projects, arguing that the limited 250‑unit supply, green‑zone status, and higher appreciation potential make the M3M plots a superior investment.  
- Regulatory context: the area is classified as a “green zone” (neither red, white nor orange), which is presented as an advantage.  
- Buyer’s intent: Baldev expressed interest in appreciation‑driven investment rather than operating a business from the plot.  

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- Alok agreed to send detailed information and documents to Baldev via WhatsApp and his personal phone number.  
- Both parties scheduled a face‑to‑face meeting in Gurgaon after Baldev’s marathon in Chandigarh, proposing the 6th or 7th of the month as suitable dates.  
- Baldev will review the sent materials and confirm the meeting time, after which he may proceed with the 50 % booking payment.
