**Main Topic / Objective**  
Discussion between Amit (client/partner based in Chandigarh) and a representative of an immigration/visa consultancy about B2B visa processing services, pricing, guarantees, and next steps for handling client cases for Canada, the United Kingdom, and Schengen countries.

**Key Points Discussed**  

1. **Service Portfolio** – The consultancy currently handles visa cases for Canada, the UK, and Schengen area (Europe). No services for Australia.  
2. **Pricing & Guarantees**

4. **Documentation Required** – One original passport and a PDF‑scanned copy of the passport are needed before any payment is taken.  
5. **Payment Flow** – Payment is to be collected from the client (referred to as “DDS”) only after the required documents are received; the consultancy will cover all filing fees.  
6. **Visa Flexibility** – The Schengen visa issued will be a multi‑country (up to 29 countries) visa, allowing the client to land in any eligible country within the zone.  
7. **Logistics & Follow‑up** –  
   - The representative will call Amit on a direct number or arrange a meeting with a manager if needed.  
   - Amit was told to keep the client’s attachments ready and may need to visit the office (location mentioned near Jalandhar bus stand).  
   - There was brief confusion about the phone number being used (call‑center vs. direct line).

2. **Payment Timing** – No payment is to be taken until the above documents are received; once verified, the client will be invoiced the agreed amount (12–16.5 lakh depending on the visa type).

4. **Office Visit (if needed)** – Amit may need to visit the consultancy’s office near Jalandhar bus stand for further processing or clarification.
