Main Topic/Objective  
The customer, Manoj Kumar Sharma, is calling to obtain the status of a refund request he submitted on the 17th and is seeking an immediate update.

Key Points Discussed  
- The refund request was placed on the 17th; the customer does not recall whether it was for the sector 48 or sector 62 location.  
- The sale was handled by a salesperson named Vikas.  
- The customer provided his contact numbers (99‑1, 55‑8, 93) for follow‑up.  
- The agent explained that refunds are normally processed within ten to fifteen days, but acknowledged possible delays due to the festival season.  
- The customer insisted that refunds should be completed in five days and demanded to see the status directly in the ZX system rather than being transferred to another team.  
- The agent asked for the name under which the refund was filed and confirmed it as Manoj Kumar Sharma.  
- The agent promised to check the system and get back to the customer.

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- The agent will locate the refund request in the ZX application, verify its current status, and report the findings.  
- The agent will call the customer back within ten minutes with the updated information.  
- If the status cannot be resolved immediately, the agent will involve a manager or the concerned team and provide the manager’s contact number to the customer.
