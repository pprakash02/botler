Main Topic/Objective  
The conversation was a sales pitch from a representative of Prasad Mehta Vyapar Nai Company to a potential client regarding auto‑vehicle advertising solutions in Jaipur and the related pricing and logistics.

Key Points Discussed  
- The representative clarified that the company is based in Delhi (Pitampura) but offers pan‑India services, including Jaipur.  
- Three types of auto advertising were described:  
  1. Vinyl wrap (18 in × 36 in) placed on the rear of the vehicle for one month at ₹100 per auto.  
  2. Multi‑permanent reflective tattoo (10 in × 24 in) that remains until the vehicle is repainted, lasting three months at ₹150 per auto.  
  3. Full‑hood Rexine cover applied on three sides of the vehicle, lasting three months at ₹550 per auto (including installation).  
- For each product the minimum order is 200 autos.  
- Cab branding was also mentioned: a full cab wrap costs ₹2,000 and lasts one month.  
- The client confirmed interest in receiving detailed information and samples via WhatsApp.

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- The sales representative will send the product samples, pricing sheets, and any additional details to the client’s WhatsApp number.  
- The client agreed to receive the information and indicated that future communication can use the same WhatsApp number.  
- No final purchase commitment was made during the call; the next step is the client’s review of the materials sent.
