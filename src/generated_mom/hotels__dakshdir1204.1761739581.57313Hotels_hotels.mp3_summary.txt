Main Topic/Objective  
The conversation is a telephone booking inquiry where the customer is trying to secure accommodation for a family of five (parents, two teenage boys, and a young daughter) at the Darshan Grand Hotel for the same day.

Key Points Discussed  
- Family composition: husband, wife, an 18‑year‑old son, a 14‑year‑old son, and a 4‑year‑old daughter.  
- Hotel room availability: only two family‑type rooms are open. One room has a double bed and a single bed; the other (the only room with two double beds) is priced higher.  
- Pricing: the base rate is ₹5,500 per night per room (including taxes). With breakfast (₹250 per adult, child not counted) the total rises to about ₹6,000 per night per room.  
- Sleeping capacity: the double‑bed/single‑bed configuration can accommodate up to five people.  
- Customer’s preference: interested in the standard family room (double + single) and wants the rate around ₹5,000–₹6,000.  
- Confirmation of details: customer repeats the guest list and asks for total cost; hotel confirms ₹6,000 per night including breakfast, but the customer later says “without any breakfast, we need it,” indicating a possible shift back to the base rate.  
- Logistics: the customer is currently outside (mention of “Chhoti Dwarka”) and asks the hotel to send the location to the phone number they are calling from.  
- Payment: the customer mentions providing an advance payment and asks to talk again in two minutes.

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- Hotel Representative to send the exact address/location of Darshan Grand Hotel to the caller’s phone number.  
- Hotel Representative to confirm the final room rate (₹5,500 without breakfast or ₹6,000 with breakfast) and prepare a booking for one family room as requested.  
- Customer to arrange the advance payment within the next few minutes (as indicated by “I’ll talk to you in two minutes”).  
- Both parties to reconfirm the booking details (room type, rate, and payment receipt) once the advance is received.
