Main Topic/Objective  
The customer called to arrange the first service check for his vehicle (initial service at 1,000 km) and sought clarification on the duration and availability of the appointment.

Key Points Discussed  
- The customer’s car has approximately 1,300 km on the odometer and is due for the initial service.  
- The customer was under the impression that the service would take only 10‑15 minutes and hoped to have it done the same day.  
- The service representative explained that the welcome service requires a full check‑up lasting about two and a half to three hours and cannot be completed in 10‑15 minutes.  
- Because the workshop was experiencing a rush, no same‑day slot was available; the earliest available slot was the next day.  
- The parties exchanged and verified the customer’s contact number (6230123214).  
- The representative offered to send a WhatsApp message with the location and also to call the customer directly. The customer indicated the number is not on WhatsApp and prefers a phone call.  
- The representative suggested arranging a pickup and drop‑off service if needed.  
- Both agreed that the representative would call the customer the following morning to confirm the appointment and any additional logistics.

Decisions Made or Action Items  
1. The service appointment will be scheduled for the next day, not today.  
2. The service representative will call the customer tomorrow morning on the confirmed number 6230123214.  
3. The representative will also send the workshop location via WhatsApp (if the customer later enables it) and discuss pickup/drop‑off arrangements during the follow‑up call.
