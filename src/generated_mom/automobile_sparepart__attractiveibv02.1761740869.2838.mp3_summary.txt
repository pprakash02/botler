Main Topic/Objective  
The conversation is a sales inquiry about replacing a car tire (size 275/18, tubeless) and related service offers, pricing, and promotional items.

Key Points Discussed  
- The customer asks for the price of a single tubeless tire, specifying the size 275/18.  
- The sales representative quotes a price of fourteen hundred rupees.  
- The representative mentions additional benefits: three‑year unconditional warranty, five‑year manufacturing warranty, and that fitting charges will be waived.  
- The customer inquires about a complimentary car wash and a gift (earbuds). The representative says a wash is not possible at the current time but can be done in the morning; the gift is available but not a permanent offer and the expiry date is unknown.  
- The customer asks about the store’s operating hours, being told to arrive before 6:30 am (or before 7:30 am for the tire).  
- Both parties confirm the tire specifications and price multiple times.

Decisions Made / Action Items  
- The customer will need to visit the store before the indicated time (by 6:30 am/7:30 am) to purchase the tire.  
- The fitting will be performed without extra charge.  
- A car wash can be scheduled for the following morning; it will not be available during the current visit.  
- The promotional earbuds are offered as a gift, but the promotion is not permanent and the end date is not provided.  
- No further commitments are made; the customer remains considering the purchase based on the provided information.
